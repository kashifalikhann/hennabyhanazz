The Artist

Henna mastery, Barcelona soul

Hanazz is a professional henna artist based in Barcelona, bringing expertise across Pakistani, Arabic, Indian and fusion styles. With a commitment to 100% natural, PPD-free henna, every design is safe, stunning and deeply personal.

From bridal mehndi for destination weddings to intimate private sessions and large-scale celebrations, Hanazz works with each client to create something uniquely theirs.
